Making A Potatoe Pie

You should eat potato pie for its comforting, savory taste and the energy it provides from carbohydrates.

How To Make The Basic Potatoe Pie

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 30-45 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Servings: 4-6

Ingredients List

  • Potatoes: About 2–2.5 lbs (1kg), peeled and cut into chunks
  • Butter: 2–4 tbsp (25–50g), plus extra for greasing
  • 1/4–1 cup (60–250ml), as needed for mashing
  • Egg: 1 large, beaten, for extra richness
  • Seasoning: Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Onion: Optional, 1 chopped medium onion or 5–6 sliced spring onions

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Place the peeled and cut potatoes in a large pot with lightly salted water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15–20 minutes, or until the potatoes are fork-tender.
  2. Step 2: Carefully drain the potatoes. Return them to the hot saucepan over very low heat for a minute to steam-dry them, which helps create fluffier mashed potatoes.
  3. Step 3: Transfer the potatoes to a large bowl. Add the butter and half of the milk, then mash until smooth or with your desired consistency. Add more milk if needed.
  4. Step 4: Stir in most of the grated cheese, the beaten egg (if using), and any other desired seasonings like chopped onions, spring onions, or mustard powder.
  5. Step 5: Place the pie in the preheated oven and bake for 20–45 minutes, depending on the crust style, until the topping is golden brown and the pie is heated through.
